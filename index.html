Bethan Cropper is a Graduate Software Engineer at THG, on the Accelerator programme. She has a PhD in Nuclear Physics from The University of Manchester (pending the viva) and has completed a six-month internship at the BBC in the AI and Machine Learning team of their R&D division. She is an excellent communicator with experience in science communication, having won "I'm a Scientist, Get Me Out of Here! and being involved with public outreach at the university and the BBC since her days as an undergraduate. Now, she is retraining for a career in software development at THG, and aims to put her natural curiosity and analytical skills to the new test of innovating for a globally renowned tech company.
